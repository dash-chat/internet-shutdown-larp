❌ The lock doesn’t budge.

“Fires are burning all over town. The internet and the phones are gone. Our relief stations can’t talk to each other — their messages must now travel in your pocket. Will you help?”

📋 What to do

  1. Scan the 4 QR posters on the wall — add each one in Dash Chat.
  2. Create a group with all 4 of them.
  3. Walk to a station and join its Wi-Fi (firefighters-larp, hospital-larp, …). Your phone picks up and delivers messages by itself.
  4. Bring each message to the station it asks for.
  5. Mobile data OFF. Forget your other Wi-Fi networks.

Checking the notice board…

This network has no internet. You can safely dismiss any sign-in screen and keep using the town network.
